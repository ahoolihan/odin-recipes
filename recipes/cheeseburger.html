Cheeseburger

Description

The below recipe is a delecious cheeseburger made from scratch.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Brioche buns
  • American burger cheese
  • Brown onion
  • Ketchup
  • American mustard
  • Dill pickles

Method

  1. Roll the patties in your hands and gently flatten to the right size. They will shrink a little while cooking, so make them slightly larger than the buns.
  2. Like a perfect steak, burger patties only need to be flipped once. Too much flipping and the meat will dry out and fall apart. Use a little oil on the hotplate and a high heat. Let a crust form on the bottom and then flip. A medium burger will be done in around eight minutes and will have a perfectly crispy shell.
  3. For the most gooey, deliciously melty cheese, add the cheese to the burger patty in the last few minutes of cooking and let it melt and completely over the patty.
  4. Place the burger patty covered in melted cheese on top of the brioche bun.
  5. Sprinkle the chopped brown onion over the top, followed by a layer of sliced pickles.
  6. Add ketchup and mustard and place the bun over the top.
